Core Features

Everything your hospital needs

A complete toolkit to digitise patient flow, staff workflow, and financial operations — without the learning curve.

Patient Management

Centralised records, medical history, visit notes, and prescriptions — all searchable and secure in one digital chart.

Appointment Scheduling

Smart calendar, reminders by SMS & WhatsApp, and real-time slot management that reduces no-shows by up to 40%.

Staff Management

Roster doctors, nurses, and admin staff with role-based access, shift tracking, and performance insights.

Billing & Invoicing

Generate GST-ready invoices, track insurance claims, and accept UPI, card, or cash payments from a single screen.

Reports & Analytics

Real-time dashboards on revenue, patient trends, and staff efficiency — turn data into confident decisions.

Cloud & Secure

Bank-grade encryption, role-based access, automated backups, and 99.9% uptime hosted on trusted Indian data centres.
